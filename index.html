Welcome to Next Dymaxion.

Next Dymaxion (word play combining Buckminster Fuller's favourite word Dymaxion with “Next Dimension”) is to be a place where anyone interested in coding, or hacking in general, can join to share experiences in their own self-learning, ask questions, discuss project ideas or new cool discoveries on an informal setting (probably over some sort of food-thing like pizzas).

We have to organize a place and a time. Hopefully we can organize something regular.

Check our Facebook Group for latest updates!
